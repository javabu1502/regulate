## 4. Description

When panic hits, your thinking brain goes offline. Regulate works with your body — because that's what actually helps.

If you have ever tried to think your way through a panic attack, you already know: it does not work. Your prefrontal cortex is offline. Breathing apps tell you to "just breathe." CBT apps ask you to challenge your thoughts. Chatbots want you to type. None of that is available to you when your nervous system has taken over.

Regulate is different. It is the only app built entirely on somatic and polyvagal principles — the science of how your body regulates itself. Instead of asking you to think, it gives you things to do with your body. Tap. Press. Orient. Shake. Breathe in patterns that directly signal safety to your vagus nerve. These are the same techniques used by Somatic Experiencing practitioners, EMDR therapists, and trauma-informed clinicians worldwide.

WHAT MAKES REGULATE DIFFERENT

-- Body-First, Always --
Every tool in Regulate works through your body, not your cognition. Bilateral tapping at clinical EMDR frequency. Physiological sighing calibrated to vagal tone research. Grounding and orienting exercises that bring you back into the present through sensation, not thought.

-- SOS Mode --
One tap. Immediate somatic tools. No sign-in, no onboarding, no paywall. When you are in crisis, Regulate meets you there — not with platitudes, but with body-based interventions that work when thinking cannot.

-- State-Matched Tools --
Your nervous system is not always in the same state, so your tools should not be either. Regulate matches interventions to where you actually are — hyperactivated, shutdown, dissociated, or somewhere in between. Different states need different responses.

-- 14 Somatic Exercises --
Bilateral tapping. Physiological sigh breathing. Box breathing. Coherence breathing. Body scanning. Orienting. Grounding through the senses. Pendulation. Container visualization. These are not generic relaxation exercises. They are clinically grounded somatic interventions, each calibrated to research-backed parameters.

-- No Gamification. No Streaks. No Points. --
Regulate is not trying to make nervous system regulation fun. It is trying to make it available. No cartoon mascots, no achievement badges, no guilt when you miss a day. Just tools that respect you as an adult doing hard work.

PRICING

Every crisis tool is free. Forever. $2.99 one-time for personalized insights — recommendations based on what works for you, session history, and pattern tracking. No subscription. No recurring charges. No surprise billing. One price. Lifetime.

PRIVACY

No accounts. No cloud. No tracking. Your data stays on your device. Period. Regulate does not know who you are, does not collect analytics, and never will. In a space where vulnerable people share their worst moments, we believe privacy is not a feature. It is an obligation.

CLINICAL FOUNDATION

Built on polyvagal theory (Stephen Porges), Somatic Experiencing (Peter Levine), and EMDR bilateral stimulation research. Every exercise parameter — tapping frequency, breath ratios, scan sequences — is calibrated to published clinical protocols, not guesswork.

Regulate is the app every trauma therapist can recommend to every client without hesitation.
